LETS PLAY

What is this?

God Hates Charades is a game where players act out vile scenes in the form of thirty seconds act outs.

Players receive points when a guesser guesses their scene. And guessers receive points for guessing.

The first player to seven points wins.

What do I do when its my turn?

When it’s your turn, draw two Actor and Scenario cards. Now combine an Actor and Scenario card to make a Scene. You will have fifteen seconds to pick your Scene. Discard the Actor and Scenario card that you didn’t choose.

What are the rules?

The only rule is you can’t say anything printed on your Actor or Scenario card.

If someone guesses the Scene, you score a point by keeping the Actor card. You give them the Scenario card because they guessed correctly.

If time runs out. Discard both of the cards in your hand. Nobody gets points. God wins.
